==== Overview

New automations and automation nodes are inactive by default. Read on to
learn how to activate your automation nodes and automation.

==== In this guide

* link:#node[Activate email nodes]
* link:#automation[Activate an automation]

[[node]]
=== Activate email nodes

Inactive email nodes will be skipped in activated automations.

To activate email nodes:

. Click the *email node*
. Click *Activate*

[[automation]]
=== Activate an automation

Inactive automations will not let new contacts trigger the automation.
To prevent new contacts from triggering an automation while allowing
existing contacts to finish flowing through the automation,
https://help.sendlane.com/article/665-how-to-pause-an-automation[pause
your automation].

To activate the automation:

. In the upper right corner, click *Active*
. Click *Activate*
